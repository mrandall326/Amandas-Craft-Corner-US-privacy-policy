Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This privacy policy describes how this application ("the App") handles information when connecting to Pinterest on behalf of the shop owner.

Who this app is for

This is a private, single-user tool used exclusively by the shop owner to automate the creation of Pinterest Pins for their Etsy digital download shop. It is not a public-facing service and does not have end-user sign-ups.

What data we access

  • Your Pinterest account information (username, account ID) — used only to verify the connection.
  • Pinterest board information — used to post Pins to the correct board.
  • OAuth access tokens — stored locally on the shop owner's private machine and never transmitted to third parties.

What we do with your data

Data accessed via the Pinterest API is used solely to create Pins that link back to Etsy listings. No data is sold, shared, rented, or disclosed to any third party. No analytics or tracking services are used.

Data storage

OAuth tokens are stored in a local .env file on the shop owner's private computer. No data is stored on external servers or cloud databases.

Data retention

Access tokens are retained only as long as they are needed to operate the App. They can be revoked at any time through the Pinterest app settings at pinterest.com/settings/security.

Third-party services

The App communicates with the Pinterest API (api.pinterest.com) and the Etsy API (openapi.etsy.com). No other third-party services receive your data.

Your rights

You may revoke the App's access to your Pinterest account at any time by visiting Pinterest Security Settings and removing the app's authorisation.
